Check-Up

General Examination:

The doctor reviews your personal and family medical history, including any chronic conditions, previous surgeries, and current medications.

Physical Examination:

A thorough physical exam where the doctor assesses vital signs (blood pressure, heart rate, temperature, respiratory rate), checks your overall physical condition, and evaluates major systems (cardiovascular, respiratory, digestive, etc.)

LABORATORY TEST Blood Tests:

Routine blood work to check various markers like blood glucose levels, cholesterol levels, liver and kidney function, and complete blood count (CBC) to detect anemia or infections.

Urinalysis:

Testing a urine sample to check for kidney function, infections, or other metabolic conditions.

Circumcision

PRE-PROCEDURE CONSULTATION Medical History Review:

The healthcare provider will review the patient's medical history, including any allergies, current medications, and previous surgeries.

Physical Examination:

A brief examination to assess the health of the penis and surrounding area, ensuring that the patient is a suitable candidate for the procedure.

PREPARATION FOR SURGERY Informed Consent:

The patient or guardians (for minors) will be required to sign an informed consent form, acknowledging understanding of the procedure and its risks.

Preoperative Instructions:

Instructions on how to prepare for the procedure, including fasting if necessary, and any medications to avoid.

Vaccination

Stay protected with our reliable vaccination services. We offer a wide range of vaccines for individuals of all ages, ensuring you and your loved ones are safeguarded against serious diseases.

Available Vaccines:

  • -Vaccinations: Protects against diseases like measles, polio, and more.
  • -Adult Vaccinations: Includes flu, hepatitis, tetanus, and others to keep you healthy.
  • -Travel Vaccines: Safeguard yourself before international travel with vaccines like yellow fever and typhoid.
  • -COVID-19 Vaccine: Stay up-to-date with COVID-19 vaccines and boosters.

How It Works:

  • -Get Vaccinated: We ensure a quick, safe, and comfortable experience.
  • -Post-Vaccination Care: Our team will guide you on what to expect after vaccination. Vaccinations are safe, effective, and critical for preventing illnesses.

Prenatal Care

Our prenatal care services are dedicated to supporting the health of both mother and baby throughout pregnancy. From your first visit, we offer comprehensive care to help ensure a safe and healthy pregnancy.

Services We Offer:

  • -Nutritional Guidance: Personalized advice to maintain a healthy diet for you and your baby.
  • -Pregnancy Education: Learn about each stage of pregnancy and what to expect during labor and delivery.

Why Prenatal Care is Important:

Receiving regular prenatal care helps reduce the risk of complications and ensures your baby’s healthy development. Our team will guide you through each stage of pregnancy with the care you need.
